## How to Unlock Hidden Potential

Before you can begin tinkering with your unit's stats, you must meet specific account milestones. The hidden potential system is not available to brand-new players; it is designed as a mid-to-late game progression feature.

### Reaching Level 18
The first barrier to entry is your account level. You must reach **Level 18** before the hidden potential interface becomes accessible. Once you hit this milestone, you need to locate the **Grand Elder (Guru)**. He is situated in the Grand Elder's area, which is conveniently located right next to the Summons area in the main hub.

### Eligible Units
Not every unit in your inventory can tap into this power. The system is restricted to high-rarity characters to ensure that resources are spent on units that provide the most value.

| Unit Category | Eligibility | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **5-Star Units** | Fully Eligible | Most standard high-tier units use this system. |
| **Unbound Units** | Fully Eligible | These are essentially Secret units with massive scaling. |
| **4-Star and Below** | Ineligible | Lower rarity units cannot access hidden potential nodes. |
| **6-Star/7-Star** | Fully Eligible | Advanced evolutions often have expanded trees. |

## Navigating the Potential Tree

When you talk to Guru and select an eligible unit, you will be presented with three distinct paths. While these paths may look different at first glance, they often share similar core nodes, eventually branching into specialized "Hidden Nodes" that offer unique utility.

### Node Types and Effects
Each node you activate requires a specific number of Skill Orbs. The further you progress down a path, the more expensive the nodes become, but the rewards scale accordingly.

*   **Range Nodes:** Increases the attack radius of the unit. This is vital for units like King Kai.
*   **Speed Nodes:** Decreases the interval between attacks (SPA), allowing for faster hits.
*   **Damage Nodes:** Provides a flat or percentage-based increase to the unit's base attack power.
*   **Crit Nodes:** Increases the 10% base chance to land a critical hit, often adding a "Marker" effect.
*   **Elemental Boost:** Enhances the damage dealt against specific enemy types (e.g., Fire vs. Water).
*   **Disruption Surge:** A rare node that can provide crowd control or debuff effects on impact.

> **Warning:** Be careful when pathing through the tree. While you can eventually unlock most nodes, the initial investment should always favor the unit's primary role (e.g., Range for supports, Damage for DPS).

## Farming Skill Orbs: The Challenge Grind

### Challenge Modes

| Challenge Difficulty | Orb Yield | Recommended Level |

## Strategic Allocation: Who to Upgrade First?

Not all units benefit equally from the hidden potential system. Since Skill Orbs are a finite resource that requires significant time to farm, you must prioritize units that offer the highest return on investment.

### The Support Priority (e.g., King Kai)
Units that provide buffs or utility often rely heavily on their range. For a unit like King Kai, upgrading damage nodes is generally a waste of resources. Instead, focus entirely on **Range Nodes**. By maximizing his reach, you ensure his buffs cover a larger portion of the map, which is far more valuable than a slight increase in his negligible personal damage.

### The DPS Priority (e.g., Aaron/Secret Units)
For your primary damage dealers, the priority shifts toward **Damage** and **Crit Chance**. 
1.  **Damage Nodes:** These provide the baseline for all other multipliers.
2.  **Crit Nodes:** A 10% chance to crit might seem small, but when combined with high speed, it drastically increases your average DPS.
3.  **Speed Nodes:** Essential for units with long animations to ensure they don't "leak" fast enemies.

### Dupe Nodes
If you have duplicate copies of a 5-star or Unbound unit, you can use them to unlock "Dupe Nodes." These are often the most powerful nodes in the tree, providing massive stat jumps or unlocking "Hidden Abilities" that can change how a unit functions entirely.

> **Tip:** Never sell your duplicate 5-star units for gold. Always save them for the Guru to unlock these high-tier potential nodes.

### Special Abilities
Some units unlock unique traits once a certain percentage of their tree is completed. These can include:
*   **Burn/Bleed Stacking:** Allowing elemental effects to stack more times than usual.
*   **Range Aura:** Providing a small range buff to nearby units.
*   **Cost Reduction:** Lowering the placement or upgrade cost of the unit in-match.

### Elemental Synergy
In the 2026 update, elemental boosts have become more prominent. If you are struggling with a specific Raid or Trial, check if your main units have unallocated potential points in Elemental nodes. A 15% boost against "Dark" or "Mechanical" enemies can be the difference between a loss and a flawless victory.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I reset my hidden potential nodes if I make a mistake?**

A: Currently, resetting nodes requires a specific "Potential Reset Token," which is occasionally available through event shops or high-tier challenge rewards. It is best to plan your path carefully before spending your orbs.

**Q: Does All Star Tower Defense X hidden potential carry over when I evolve a unit?**

A: Yes, in most cases, the potential progress is maintained or converted into the new unit's tree. However, some 6-star evolutions expand the tree, requiring additional orbs to reach the new maximum potential.

**Q: Are Skill Orbs shared between all units?**

A: Skill Orbs are a global currency tied to your account. You can farm them using one team and spend them to upgrade an entirely different unit.

**Q: How many Skill Orbs does it take to max out a 5-star unit?**

A: While the exact number varies by unit, most 5-star characters require between 1,500 and 2,500 Skill Orbs to fully complete all three paths and unlock the final hidden node.
