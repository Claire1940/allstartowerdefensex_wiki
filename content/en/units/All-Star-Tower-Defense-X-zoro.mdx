### Achieving 100% Crit Rate

One of the most vital aspects of Zoro's build is reaching 100% crit rate when a boss is in range. This is achieved through a combination of relic substats and his passive ability.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Relic Substats for Crit Rate">
    Aim for a total of **25% crit rate from all relic substats combined**. For example:
    - 10% from Body
    - 7.5% from Legs
    - 7.5% from Head
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Warlord Set Bonus">
    As a "Peace" unit, Zoro gains an additional **10% crit rate** from the Warlord set bonus. This brings your total to 35% from relics and set bonus.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Pirate Hunter Passive">
    When a boss enters Zoro's range, his "Pirate Hunter" passive grants him **50% crit rate**. With his E4 upgrade, this increases to **65% crit rate**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Total Crit Rate Calculation">
    Combining the 35% from relics/set and the 65% from his passive (with E4) results in a perfect **100% crit rate** against bosses. This ensures every hit on a boss is a critical hit, maximizing his damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>
