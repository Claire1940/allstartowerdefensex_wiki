## Best Economy Build Orders for Story and Infinite

A big part of mastering **All Star Tower Defense X farm units** is not just which unit you equip, but **when** you invest.

### Story Mode Build Order (short and efficient)
1. Place one cheap DPS unit first (don’t greed economy too hard).
2. Add your farm unit once wave pressure is controlled.
3. Upgrade farm only to the breakpoint needed for your target clear time.
4. Push DPS upgrades before over-investing into late-game farm tiers.

### Infinite Mode Build Order (scaling focused)
1. Secure minimum wave clear with efficient DPS.
2. Place Bulma as early as safely possible.
3. Alternate between survival upgrades and economy upgrades.
4. Hit mid-game economy spike, then front-load major DPS scaling.
5. Transition to support/CC while income carries upgrade costs.

| Mode | Opening Priority | Farm Priority | Risk Level | Recommended Farm |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Story** | Early damage consistency | Controlled, limited | Medium | 4★ farm or light Bulma |
| **Infinite** | Survive + scale | **Aggressive economy growth** | High | **Bulma core** |
| Event grind | Fast reset speed | Minimal over-upgrading | Low-Med | Cheap farm core |

> **Warning:** Over-greeding farm upgrades before your lane control is stable is the fastest way to throw a run, even with the best economy unit.

## Upgrade Planning, Stat Rolls, and Team Slot Strategy

In 2026, good economy players treat farm units as part of the full team plan, not a standalone pick. Here’s how to optimize:

### 1) Economy stat focus
For money units, income-related stat quality matters far more than combat stats. If your rolls offer stronger money scaling, take it unless your run type is very short.

### 2) Team slot planning
Your farm slot competes with:
- Main DPS
- Air coverage
- Boss burst
- Slow/stun support
- Utility

That means your economy choice must fit your roster’s total balance.

| Team Slot | Must Have? | Why it matters with farm units |
|---|---|---|
| Main DPS | **Yes** | Keeps waves controlled while you eco |

### 3) Placement limits and hidden efficiency
Even when one farm unit has better theoretical income, placement count limits can shift short-term value. That’s why some players still prefer old economy picks in short sessions.

### 4) Don’t ignore map length
Longer maps and longer wave structures increase Bulma’s effective value. Short maps reduce time to realize full scaling.

## Common Farm Mistakes (and Fast Fixes)

Players researching **All Star Tower Defense X farm units** usually make the same mistakes. Fix these and your consistency jumps fast:

- **Mistake:** Placing farm before lane safety  
  **Fix:** First secure one reliable clear unit, then eco.

- **Mistake:** Full eco tunnel vision  
  **Fix:** Alternate farm and defense upgrades by wave pressure.

- **Mistake:** Copy-pasting Infinite strategy into Story  
  **Fix:** In Story, favor quicker ROI and fewer deep farm upgrades.

- **Mistake:** Ignoring opportunity cost  
  **Fix:** Every farm upgrade delays damage/support timing—plan breakpoints.

- **Mistake:** Judging units only by max income screenshots  
  **Fix:** Compare **time-to-profit**, not just final income.

> **Tip:** A “worse” farm unit with cleaner early timing can outperform in short matches because you reach boss-kill upgrades earlier.
