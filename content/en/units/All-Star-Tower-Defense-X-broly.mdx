<Section icon={Map} title="Evolution Requirements">

Evolving Borul into his Super form is a multi-step process that demands specific farming. You cannot pull the ascended form directly from the banner; it must be crafted.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Base Units">
    Collect 3 base Borul units through standard summoning sessions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect Materials">
    Farm 5 Z-Balls (Stone), 4 Kai Swords, 3 Insect DNA, and various Ghost units (Green and Rainbow).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ascension">
    Visit the evolution NPC with all required items to finalize the transformation into Super Borul.
  </Step>
</Steps>
