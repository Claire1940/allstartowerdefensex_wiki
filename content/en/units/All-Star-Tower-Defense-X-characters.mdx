## Team Building: Role Balance, Synergies, and Placement Logic

When building teams in 2026, think in this pattern:

- **1 economy unit**
- **2 wave clear units** (AoE focus)
- **2 boss killers** (single target or burst)
- **1 flex slot** (synergy combo, debuff, or map-specific pick)

This structure works across story, challenges, and early endless.

### Recommended Team Blueprint

| Slot | Role | What to Look For |
|---|---|---|
| **Slot 1** | Economy | Low deploy cost + strong income scaling |
| **Slot 2** | Early AoE | Fast attack cycle for wave control |
| **Slot 3** | Mid-game AoE | Better range or wider cone/line damage |
| **Slot 4** | Boss DPS | High single-target burst |
| **Slot 5** | Utility/Debuff | Mark, penetration, shield interaction |
| **Slot 6** | Synergy or backup DPS | Pair unit or all-purpose closer |

### Positioning Rules That Improve Every Run

- Place economy first unless wave pressure is extreme.
- Use cheap AoE near entry to stabilize early leaks.
- Keep your highest single-target DPS near late-path curves for max hit uptime.
- Avoid stacking all units in one lane unless map pathing supports it.
- Save one placement slot for emergency adaptation.

Many **All Star Tower Defense X characters** also get better through pair mechanics. If two linked units can combine or amplify effects, the value can surpass raw solo stats.

> **Warning:** Don’t reroll traits on every unit at once. Lock your best trait results and prioritize units you actually field in your main six.
