<Section icon={Swords} title="Ascension and Abilities of Gojo (Mysterious X)">
Ascending Gojo (Mysterious X) transforms his combat effectiveness significantly. The ascended form not only boosts his base stats but also introduces a powerful new ability: Hollow Per, a line AoE attack. The most impactful addition, however, is his Domain Expansion.

The Domain Expansion ability allows Gojo to stop all enemy movements within his range for 3 seconds. This provides crucial crowd control, giving other units time to deal damage or reset cooldowns. The cooldown for this ability is 7 seconds after the stun ends, and notably, there is no global cooldown, meaning multiple Gojos can chain their Domain Expansions.

<Callout type="warning" title="Stun Immunity Consideration">
While Gojo's time stop is incredibly powerful, be mindful of enemies with stun immunity or those that can quickly bypass his range. Positioning and supporting units are key to maximizing his effectiveness.
</Callout>
