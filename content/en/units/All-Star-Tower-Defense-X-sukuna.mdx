<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire 6-Star Sukuna">
    First, you need to pull the 6-star version of Sukuna from banners. This can require considerable Stardust and luck. Patience is key during this phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Materials for 7-Star Evolution">
    The primary material needed is the **Cursed Finger**, which is obtained from the Malevolent Shrine raid. You'll need to complete this raid multiple times to collect enough.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate the Evolution">
    Once you have the necessary Cursed Fingers, go to the evolution menu and transform your 6-star Sukuna into his 7-star "Strongest Demon" form.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Achieve 6,666 Kills">
    To unlock his final model and blessing, Sukuna needs to accumulate 6,666 kills. This is best done by farming in Infinite Mode or other high-wave stages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Equip Blessing">
    After reaching the kill count, equip his unique blessing to further enhance his stats and unlock his full visual potential.
  </Step>
</Steps>
