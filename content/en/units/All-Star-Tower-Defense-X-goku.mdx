<Section icon={Target} title="Goku's Initial Evolution: Super Koku and Super God Koku">
The first step in enhancing your Goku unit is evolving him into Super Koku, and then further into Super God Koku. Each stage requires a specific set of materials that you'll need to farm through various game modes.

### Evolution to Super Koku

| Required Units/Items | Quantity | Source | Notes |
| :------------------- | :------- | :----- | :---- |
| **Koku** (base unit) | 5        | Summon | Can be obtained as a starter unit. |
| **Z Ball (Stone)**   | 3        | Story Mode, Raids | Essential for the first evolution stage. |
| **Kai Sword**        | 2        | Story Mode, Challenges | Story 3 Challenge/Expert is a good source. |
| **Ghost I (Blue)**   | 2        | Raids, Daily Challenges | Common drop. |
| **Ghost I (Rainbow)**| 2        | Raids, Daily Challenges | Slightly rarer. |

<Callout type="info">
The base Koku unit itself has a deployment cost of 450 and can be upgraded four times, reaching a total cost of 3,750. Its attack type changes from AoE (Line) to EAoE upon certain upgrades, making it a versatile ground unit.
</Callout>

### Evolution to Super God Koku

After obtaining Super Koku, the next significant evolution is to Super God Koku (Son Goku SSJ God). This transformation drastically boosts his stats and abilities.

| Required Units/Items | Quantity | Source | Notes |
| :------------------- | :------- | :----- | :---- |
| **Super Koku**       | 1        | Previous Evolution | The evolved form of Koku. |
| **Z Ball**           | 1        | Story 3, Challenges | Often found in Story Mode Chapter 3 or specific challenges. |
| **Kai Sword**        | 1        | Story 3, Challenges | Same as above. |
| **Insect DNA**       | 1        | Story 3 Challenge/Expert | Crucial material, often found in harder difficulties. |
| **Ghost 2**          | 1        | Raids, Daily Challenges | Higher tier Ghost unit. |
| **Ghost 1**          | 1        | Raids, Daily Challenges | Also needed. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Farming Difficulty">
Farming for specific evolution materials like Kai Sword and Insect DNA can be time-consuming, especially if they are tied to higher difficulty story modes or challenges. Be prepared for multiple runs.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Ascending Goku to Ultra Instinct Power">
Ascension is where Goku truly shines, transforming into the immensely powerful Ultra Instinct Goku. This form is known for its devastating damage and unique abilities. However, the ascension process is complex and requires dedication.

### Ascension Requirements for Ultra Instinct Goku

| Required Materials       | Quantity | Source                                     | Notes                                                              |
| :----------------------- | :------- | :----------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Super God Koku**       | 1        | Previous Evolution                         | The fully evolved form of Goku.                                    |
| **Ghost 3 (Rainbow)**    | 2        | Expert Challenges (30% drop rate), Raids   | A rare drop, requiring persistence.                                |
| **Insect DNA**           | 5        | Story 3 Challenge/Expert                   | High quantity needed, focus farming on this.                       |
| **Ghost 1 (Red)**        | 5        | Raids, Daily Challenges                    | Specific color variant of Ghost.                                   |
| **Training Pole**        | 5        | Goku's Challenge (Talk to Goku in lobby)   | Complete his specific challenge five times.                        |
| **Jiren (6-star version)**| 1        | Limited-time Banner Summon               | **Extremely rare**, often requires luck and significant summons.  |
| **Instinct Evo materials**| 3        | Tournament of Power Raids (Top 5, Top 2)   | Specific raid drops for Ultra Instinct.                            |

<Callout type="warning" title="Jiren Acquisition">
Acquiring the 6-star version of Jiren is often the most challenging part of getting Ultra Instinct Goku. Jiren typically appears on limited-time banners, making strategic summoning and resource saving essential. Missing Jiren means you cannot complete the Ultra Instinct evolution.
</Callout>

### Strategy for Tournament of Power Raids

The Tournament of Power raids are crucial for obtaining Instinct Evo materials. These raids can be quite difficult, especially when attempting them solo.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Strategic Planning and Resource Management">
Successfully obtaining and maximizing Goku requires careful planning and efficient resource management. This includes not just farming specific items but also making smart decisions about your in-game currency and summoning.

### Key Strategies for Success

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize Summoning">
    Save your gems and summon tickets for banners featuring Goku or the elusive 6-star Jiren. Avoid spending resources on less critical units if your goal is Ultra Instinct Goku.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Efficient Farming">
    Identify the specific story modes, challenges, and raids that drop the required evolution and ascension materials. Prioritize farming these stages, even if it means multiple runs. Utilize auto-clickers if allowed by game rules to streamline repetitive tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Team Up for Raids">
    Many of the harder raids, especially the Tournament of Power, are significantly easier with a team. Join official All Star Tower Defense X Discord servers or communities to find reliable teammates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Monitor Banners">
    Keep a close eye on the in-game banner rotation for Jiren. This unit is often the bottleneck for Ultra Instinct Goku, so check frequently and be ready to summon when he appears.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Invest in Buffs">
    Once you have Ultra Instinct Goku, invest in support units that can further amplify his damage. Units that provide attack buffs or reduce enemy defenses can make him even more overpowered.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
The process of evolving and ascending Goku can be a long grind, requiring hundreds of summons and numerous raid attempts. Persistence and a solid strategy are your greatest allies.
