## Item Types You Should Track Every Session

Think of **All Star Tower Defense X items** in three buckets: guaranteed progression, RNG progression, and opportunity progression.

### 1) Guaranteed progression items
These are materials that directly and predictably increase power (XP feed, evolution mats, standard currencies).

### 2) RNG progression items
Trait rerolls or drop-based rare materials. These can create big jumps, but results vary.

### 3) Opportunity progression items
Limited tokens from events, time-limited shops, and rotating challenges. Missing these can set your account back for weeks.

| Bucket | Examples | Best Use Timing | Common Mistake |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Guaranteed** | XP materials, core evo mats | Spend continuously on main team | Hoarding too long and stalling progression |
| **RNG** | Trait reroll items | Use after unit is fully leveled/evolved | Rolling traits on unfinished units |
| **Opportunity** | Event tokens, limited currencies | Spend before rotation ends | Saving past event expiration |

This structure keeps your **All Star Tower Defense X items** from getting scattered across half-built units.

## Inventory Management Rules That Save Months of Progress

Inventory discipline is underrated. Use these rules:

### Rule A: Keep a protected reserve
Always hold enough materials for one emergency upgrade cycle (level + partial trait + key evo component). This prevents being locked out when new content drops.

### Rule B: Separate farming budget vs. optimization budget
- Farming budget: guaranteed progression items used daily  
- Optimization budget: RNG items used only on finished units

### Rule C: Track opportunity costs
If spending today delays your next evolution by several days, it might be a bad trade.

| Management Rule | Practical Threshold | Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| **Protected reserve** | 15–25% of core resources | Flexibility during updates |
| **RNG budget cap** | Max 20–30% weekly spend | Prevents reroll sinkholes |
| **Unit focus cap** | 1–2 active upgrades at once | Faster power spikes |

These habits keep your **All Star Tower Defense X items** strategy stable, even when banner luck is bad.
