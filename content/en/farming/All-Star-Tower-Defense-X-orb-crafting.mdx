## Efficient farming loop for 2026

To get consistent outcomes from **All Star Tower Defense X orb crafting**, run a repeatable loop with clear checkpoints. This avoids “busy farming” with low conversion into real power.

| Time Block | Action | Goal |

| **5–25 min** | Farm Mode run(s) | Material accumulation |

| **30–40 min** | Bulma crafting visit | Convert mats into progress |
| **40–45 min** | Unit testing run | Validate orb impact |

This loop works for short sessions and scales well for longer grinds.

### Resource discipline rules

1. **Set a craft target before farming**  
   Don’t start runs without a destination.

2. **Craft in milestones**  
   Craft when you hit meaningful thresholds, not every tiny increment.

3. **Test after crafting**  
   Validate whether your new orb changed your weak point.

4. **Adjust team based on results**  
   Keep what works, rotate what doesn’t.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Farming without testing is a hidden time sink. If you don’t evaluate each craft, you may keep upgrading the wrong slot.

| Mistake | Why It Hurts | Fix |
