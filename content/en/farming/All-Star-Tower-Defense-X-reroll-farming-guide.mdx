## Shop Resets and Guaranteed Sources You Should Never Skip

A huge part of efficient farming is claiming capped rewards before they expire. Many players do this backward and lose value.

### 1) Redeem codes early in account progression
Codes are often the easiest reroll injection, but some can require certain level milestones. If one does not work yet, level naturally and retry.

### 2) Clear Trial Shop stock every reset cycle
Trial shops typically let you buy a fixed number of Trait Burners using trial coins. In recent routing, this is one of the highest priority weekly cash-outs.

### 3) Clear Portal/Event Shop stock
When portal shops are active, they can add a large reroll total. Treat these like limited inventory: buy out rerolls before optional cosmetics or low-priority boosts.

| Shop Type | Typical Currency | Suggested Buy Order | Reset Behavior |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Trial Shop** | Trial coins | **Trait Burners → lock/utility items** | **Periodic weekly restock** |
| **Portal/Event Shop** | Event portal currency | **Trait Burners first** | **Event-limited and/or periodic** |
| **Dungeon/Mode Shop** | Mode-specific coins | **Rerolls if efficient** | Varies by mode |

> **💡 Tip:** If your goal is trait rolling, treat every coin as “reroll currency” until shops are cleared. This mindset alone improves progression speed.

## Best Active Farming Loop (Highest Hands-On Value)

For active players, event mission chains are typically your strongest route. The efficient approach is not “do everything once.” It is “repeat the fastest guaranteed tiers first, then fill downtime.”

### Recommended active rotation
1. **Claim quick-reset missions first** (hourly or near-hourly).
2. **Run guaranteed-drop tasks** before chance-based tasks.
3. **Clear medium-reset mission tiers** (multi-hour windows).
4. **Only then run long maps** like Infinite if you still have session time.

In recent mission structures, lower-to-mid event ranks can provide steady guaranteed burners in short clears, while higher ranks give better burst rewards but may require stronger comps and longer clears.

| Mission Tier Style | Reroll Pattern | Best For | Clear Speed |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Hourly low-tier missions** | **Guaranteed small drops** | Consistent farming loops | Fast |
| **Mid-tier missions** | Better per-clear rewards | Players with stable teams | Medium |
| **Boss-tier missions** | Higher burst rewards | Strong rosters, active play | Medium/slow |
| **Infinite-style mission** | Opportunity value only | Overflow time | Slow |

A strong session template:
- Start by checking all mission timers.
- Burn through the shortest guaranteed missions.
- Reclaim and repeat while an AFK system (like expeditions) runs in parallel.
- Reinvest rerolls only after your mission board is dry.

This is the heart of a practical **All Star Tower Defense X reroll farming guide**: run guaranteed short timers first, not the longest content first.

## AFK Strategy: Expedition Setup That Actually Works

If you cannot actively spam missions, expeditions are your background engine. The goal is lowering expedition duration while keeping reroll-relevant mission choices active.

### Build your expedition roster correctly
- Use your strongest eligible units (high level, upgraded trees).
- Prioritize teams that reduce completion times.
- Keep six slots filled with reliable, optimized picks.
- Recheck after every major unit upgrade.

| Expedition Approach | Strength | Weakness | Use Case |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Shortest mission spam** | Frequent reward checks | Smaller individual payouts | Best for frequent logins/macros |
| **Long mission cycles** | Bigger single claim windows | Slower feedback | Best when you are fully offline |
| **Mixed cycle rotation** | Flexible timing | More manual management | Hybrid players |

> **💡 Tip:** Pair active event runs with expedition refreshes. Launch expedition → clear 1–2 quick missions → return to claim/reset expedition. This doubles efficiency without needing nonstop micromanagement.

For players using automated routines, this is where most reroll gains accumulate over days, not hours.

| Player Type | Daily Time | Must-Do Checklist | Secondary Tasks |

- **Day 1–2:** Push currency modes needed for shop buyouts.

This structure keeps your account ahead even if event balance changes. A well-built **All Star Tower Defense X reroll farming guide** is less about one “secret mode” and more about repeatable priority discipline.
