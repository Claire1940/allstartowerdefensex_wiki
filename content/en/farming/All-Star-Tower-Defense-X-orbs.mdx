## Best Farming Loop for All Star Tower Defense X orbs (Session Plan)

For efficiency, do not improvise every run. Use a fixed loop so your progress stays consistent.

### 45-Minute Orb Session Template

| Time Block | Goal | Recommended Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **0-5 min** | Setup | Check team, orb needs, and entry mode |
| **5-25 min** | Main farming | Prioritize clean clears over risky pushes |
| **25-35 min** | Second pass | Repeat quickest reliable route |
| **35-40 min** | Crafting stop | Return to Bulma, craft if possible |
| **40-45 min** | Loadout review | Equip, test, and plan next session |

This method is simple, but it works because it removes downtime. Players who bounce between random activities often “play longer” but progress slower.

### Team-building priorities for orb farming

- Use units you can deploy consistently, not just flashy picks.
- Balance early defense and wave control so you avoid resets.
- Keep one flexible slot for testing orb-boosted units.
- Favor stable clear speed over hard greed strategies.

> **Warning:** Overextending for one more wave can reduce hourly efficiency if it causes frequent failures. Reliable completion is usually better for long-term orb growth.

## Orb Crafting Priorities: What to Build First

Because **All Star Tower Defense X orbs** currently start from a smaller craft pool, prioritize by role impact rather than hype.

A practical order:
1. Craft for your most-used carry unit first.
2. Craft for your core support or wave-control unit second.
3. Build situational options after your baseline team feels stable.

| Priority Tier | Where to Use It | Why It’s Efficient |
|---|---|---|
| **Tier 1: Main carry slot** | Your primary damage dealer | Biggest immediate account impact |
| **Tier 2: Core control/support** | Units you deploy every run | Improves consistency and safety |
| **Tier 3: Niche/situational** | Mode-specific experiments | Useful later, lower early value |

If you are unsure where to invest, ask one question: *Which unit appears in nearly every successful run I do?* That unit usually gets first orb investment.

### Common crafting mistakes to avoid

- Crafting for units you rarely deploy.
- Ignoring farm consistency while chasing advanced setups.
- Splitting resources across too many units too early.
- Forgetting to re-test loadout after each craft.

The whole point of **All Star Tower Defense X orbs** progression is compounding value. Focused upgrades beat scattered upgrades nearly every time.

## Advanced Progression Tips for Mid-Game Players

Once your first orb setup is done, optimization matters more than raw grind hours.

### 1) Track runs by outcome, not by time played
Use simple notes:
- Run success/fail
- Clear speed
- Resource gain estimate
- Which unit loadout was used

This helps you identify your best route quickly.

### 2) Create two loadouts
- **Farm loadout:** built for reliable resource generation.
- **Push loadout:** built for tougher attempts and testing limits.

Switching loadouts prevents “one-size-fits-none” team problems.

### 3) Sync orb crafting with mode goals
If your next target is longer Infinite attempts, craft around scaling and survivability.  
If your target is farming speed, craft around deployment tempo and consistent wave clears.

### 4) Stay update-aware
ASTD-style games can shift quickly. Keep an eye on official channels for changes:
- mode access,
- crafting requirements,
- orb pool expansion.

You can monitor platform updates through the [official Roblox experience hub](https://www.roblox.com/discover/).
