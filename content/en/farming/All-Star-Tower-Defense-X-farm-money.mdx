## ROI Mindset: Simple Math That Improves Every Run

You don’t need exact spreadsheets to improve your farming. Use quick ROI checkpoints:

- **Placement ROI:** How many waves to recover placement cost?
- **Upgrade ROI:** Does this farm upgrade return faster than a DPS upgrade prevents leaks?
- **Match Horizon:** How many waves remain? Long horizon favors scaling farms.

| Decision Check | Good Sign | Bad Sign | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Place farm now?** | No leak risk for 2+ waves | Lane almost collapsing | Delay farm, boost defense |
| **Upgrade farm now?** | Defense comfortably clears | Boss spike incoming | Hold cash for DPS |
| **Commit to max farm?** | Long run expected | Run likely ending soon | Spend on damage instead |

This is where most **All Star Tower Defense X farm money** gains happen: making better decisions under pressure, not just copying a loadout.
