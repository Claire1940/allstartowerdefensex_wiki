### Setting Up Your Macro
To start your **All Star Tower Defense X gem farm** automation, you will need a reliable Infinite Mode team. Units like **Madara (6-Star)** or **Gohan (Super Alternative)** are excellent for early-to-mid wave clearing without requiring constant interaction.

1. **Clear Inventory:** Ensure you have ample space for XP units (ghosts) that drop during the run.
2. **Disable Blessings:** Unequip all unit blessings to ensure your character's position remains static during the macro recording.
3. **Recording the Loop:** Start your recording at the main menu. Click "Infinite Mode," select "Extreme," and place your primary damage dealer and a farm unit (like Bulma or Idol).
4. **Auto-Replay:** Enable the "Auto Replay" setting in the ASTD settings menu. This ensures the game restarts the stage automatically upon defeat.
5. **The Evolution Loop:** The real gem profit comes from the "Evolve Units" quest. Every 5 units you evolve grants 25 gems. By macroing Infinite Mode, you collect hundreds of XP units which can then be evolved to trigger this reward repeatedly.

## The Prestige System: A 10,000 Gem Windfall

Once you reach Level 127, you unlock the ability to Prestige. This is a significant milestone that resets your level but grants massive rewards. To find the Prestige NPC, head to World 1, go toward the Tournament section, and jump onto the mountains to find **Aaron Yeager**.

When you interact with him, you are given three "Wishes." For those focused on the **All Star Tower Defense X gem farm**, the choice is clear: **Wish for Riches**.

*   **Reward:** 10,000 Gems, 500 Stardust, and 10,000 Coins.
*   **Alternative (Wish for Power):** Grants a specific high-tier unit.
*   **Alternative (Wish for Relic):** Grants a specialized Orb (e.g., Initial Range Orb).

> **⚠️ Warning:** Prestige resets your account level. While you keep your units and items, ensure you don't need a high level for specific raids or trials before committing to the reset.

## Daily Infinite Trials (Ticket System)

Every day, players receive one **Infinite Trial Ticket**. This ticket allows entry into a specialized trial in World 1. Completing this trial on Extreme difficulty is perhaps the most efficient 15-minute activity you can do.

By selecting the **"Gems and Coins"** path within the trial, you can earn approximately **1,000 gems per run**. The enemies at the end of the trial can reach upwards of 8 million health, so ensure your team is upgraded. If Extreme is too difficult, the Normal mode still provides a respectable 300 gems.
