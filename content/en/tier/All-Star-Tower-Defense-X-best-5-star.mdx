Ishtar is a tactical unit. Her dynamic AoE targeting is excellent on maps where enemy pathing is predictable or when you need damage from safe positions. She can be placed away from stun zones and still pressure the main route.

**Best use case:** controlled AoE at spawn points/choke zones.

### 5) Sukuna (5★)

Sukuna’s bleed utility gives him extra relevance in regen-heavy encounters. Even when not your top burst damage option, the anti-regen pressure and hill slot value make him a smart long-term pickup.

**Best use case:** anti-regen coverage with consistent hill damage.
