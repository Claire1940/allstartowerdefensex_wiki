## Mode-specific picks: Story, Raid, and Reverse

The reason many guides fail is they rank units globally without context. This **All Star Tower Defense X unit tier list** works better if you adapt it by mode.

### Story / Standard progression
- Prioritize low setup carries and one solid lane stabilizer.
- Bring at least one unit that can handle tanky waves without heavy micromanagement.

### Raid content
- Boss pressure matters more than early wave speed.
- Invest earlier in your primary scaling DPS and avoid over-placing weak openers.

### Reverse Mode
- Economy limitations change priorities.
- Units that provide immediate impact on deployment are stronger than slow-ramp specialists.

| Mode | Best Unit Profile | Common Mistake | Fix |

| **Raid** | High boss DPS with sustain | Delaying carry upgrades | Upgrade core unit earlier |
| **Reverse** | Immediate impact and efficient re-placement | Greedy upgrade path | Cycle placements and sell timing |
