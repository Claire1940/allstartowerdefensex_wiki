## Best unit roles you should build first

If your goal is progression, build by **role order**, not by banner hype.

### 1) Main carry DPS
Start with one hyper-carry that scales into boss waves (e.g., Sung Jin Woo path, Frieren-type DPS modes, evolved Yuta-type anchors).

### 2) Economy
A strong economy unit is still one of the biggest win-rate boosters in ASTDX-style gameplay loops. Faster income means earlier upgrades and fewer failures.

### 3) Crowd control / debuff support
Units that apply slow, stun, vulnerability, or damage amplification can outperform a second mediocre DPS slot.

### 4) Burst/nuke utility
Boss phases now often demand timed burst. Keep at least one reliable active ability unit.

### 5) Flex slot
Swap this based on map needs: anti-air, extra support, shield break, or boss-focused specialist.

| Role | Priority | Recommended Investment |
|---|---|---|
| **Main Carry** | **Highest** | Max level, best trait, best relic set |
| **Economy** | **Very High** | Early placement consistency over pure stats |
| **Support CC/Debuff** | **High** | Upgrade enough to unlock core passive/ability |
| **Burst/Nuke** | **Medium-High** | Skill timing matters more than full max |
| **Flex Counterpick** | **Medium** | Rotate by map and enemy profile |

> **Warning:** Over-stacking damage dealers without support causes many late-wave losses. One debuff slot can outperform your third DPS unit.
