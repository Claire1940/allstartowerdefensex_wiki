## Strategies for Successful Trading

Trading is the lifeblood of the ASTD community. To effectively use the **All Star Tower Defense X value list**, you must understand the psychology of the trade hub. 

*   **Avoid "Dead" Units:** Some units may have high raw stats but are "dead" in the market because they lack a 7-star evolution path. Always prioritize units that have future-proofing.
*   **Watch the Banners:** When a character like Sukuna or Ego Vegeta returns to the Stardust Banner, their trade value temporarily dips. This is the best time to buy. Conversely, sell your duplicates just before a major holiday event when demand spikes.
*   **The Discord Advantage:** Since the game lacks an automated global matchmaking system for raids, the Discord server acts as the secondary market. Units that are "required" for the current trending raid will always command a premium.

> **💡 Tip:** Always check the "Sub-Announcements" in the official Discord. Developers often drop hints about which units might receive a buff, which can cause their value to skyrocket overnight.

## Story Mode Progress and Unit Requirements

With 34 chapters and six stages each, Story Mode is a daunting task for new players. Many of the units on the **All Star Tower Defense X value list** are actually rewards or evolutions from completing these stages.

1.  **Chapter 1-10:** Can be cleared with basic 5-star units and a decent farm.
2.  **Chapter 11-25:** Requires at least one 6-star DPS and a slow/stun unit like Blackbeard or Grey.
3.  **Chapter 26-34:** These stages are "Godly" tier. You will need 7-star units like UI Goku or Gear 5 Luffy to manage the enemy health scaling.

The difficulty curve is steep, and players often get stuck around Chapter 20. This is why "carry" services—where high-level players help lower-level players for units or gems—are a common part of the 2026 economy.
