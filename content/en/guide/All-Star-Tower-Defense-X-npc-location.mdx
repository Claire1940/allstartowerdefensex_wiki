## Detailed Area-by-Area Location Guide

### 1) AFK and Snakeway Zone
Start near spawn and rotate toward AFK first. Vegeta is close by, making him easy to include at almost no travel cost. Then swing toward Snakeway for the Trial Shop NPC. This zone is usually low traffic and quick to clear.

### 2) Story Front and Mountain Cluster
This is one of the densest clusters for useful NPCs. You can catch Gojo, Sukuna, and Ulquiorra along main movement lanes. Kokushibo is up high near the mountain by Story, while Zoro sits behind Story.

Blackbeard appears on a rock to your right side when entering the broader hub path. In many accounts, Blackbeard-related tasks feel more impactful than random side NPC checks, so do this early.

### 3) Outfit Shop Rear
For many players, this is the most searched **All Star Tower Defense X npc location** area because Sung Jin-Woo is placed behind the Outfit shop. If you skip this zone, you might delay critical quest progress without realizing it.

Other themed NPCs in this section are useful, but Sung Jin-Woo should be your anchor checkpoint each loop.

### 4) Story Entry Side and Left Flank
Near Story you’ll find Naruto (Sage Mode), Sasuke, and to the left side path, Goku. This is a practical checkpoint before starting mission queues.

### 5) Summon Mega-Cluster
Summon is crowded and easy to misread. Split it into three mini-zones:

- **Front/Central:** Luffy, Elder, Rama  
- **Behind Summon:** Eren, Frieren, Shirou  
- **Right Wing:** Z Broly and Super Broly  

Then finish at Trade area for Nami, with Dio and Ichigo nearby. If you do this in a fixed order, you stop losing time to backtracking.
