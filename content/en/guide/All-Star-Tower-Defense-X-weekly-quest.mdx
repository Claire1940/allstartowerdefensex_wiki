## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to finish the All Star Tower Defense X weekly quest in 2026?**

A: Start every session with the daily challenge, then clear 2–4 rotating challenges, and finish with one progression mode (hard story, endless, or infinite). This gives a balanced material flow and better weekly consistency than single-mode farming.

**Q: How many times should I run infinite for weekly progress?**

A: For most players, 2–4 focused infinite sessions per week is enough. If you need XP items or gem top-ups, increase that number, but don’t let infinite replace daily and rotating challenge clears.

**Q: Should I spend gems during my weekly quest routine or save them?**

A: Save by default, then spend with a purpose (target unit, trait plan, or progression bottleneck). Random spending can delay important upgrades tied to the All Star Tower Defense X weekly quest cycle.

**Q: Does PvP help with weekly progression?**

A: Yes, but it’s a secondary track for many players. PvP can add useful currency and shop value, especially once your core PvE team is stable. If your account is new, prioritize PvE progression first.
