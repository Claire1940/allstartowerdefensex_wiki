Reaching Wave 100+ in the **All Star Tower Defense X element** leaderboard requires more than just high-damage units; it requires precise timing and resource management. The "Megumin Strategy" is a popular high-risk, high-reward tactic. Megumin deals massive explosive damage (up to 125 million per hit at max level) but has an incredibly long SPA (Seconds Per Attack).

In the late game (Wave 90+), enemies like "Cloners" can quickly overwhelm your defenses. This is where the manual activation of units like Megumin becomes vital. You must "pop" the explosion exactly when the highest HP enemies are clustered. If you mistime the explosion, the long cooldown will leave a gap in your defense that even a 7-star Katakuri cannot fill.

<Callout type="info" title="Damage Comparison">
While Megumin hits for 125M, her slow SPA means her actual DPS might be lower than a maxed Ace in certain scenarios. Use Megumin as a "finisher" rather than a primary wave clearer.
