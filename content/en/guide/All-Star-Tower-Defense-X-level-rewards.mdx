## Troubleshooting checklist for missing or delayed rewards

Not every issue is account eligibility. Sometimes rewards feel missing due to claim flow, UI confusion, or session sync delays.

| Problem | Likely Cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Reward not visible after leveling | UI not refreshed | Rejoin server and re-check reward log |
| Claimed but not in inventory | Delayed sync | Wait briefly, then relog |
| Legacy cosmetic missing | Account not eligible for year window | Verify historical activity timeline |

| Progress feels slow | Inefficient mode selection | Shift to repeatable clears with lower fail rate |

### Quick diagnostic flow

1. Confirm what type of reward is missing (level, event, or OG legacy).  
2. Verify your last successful level milestone and claim step.  
3. Rejoin and check inventory/profile tabs again.  
4. Compare your account history to legacy window requirements.  
5. If unresolved, submit a concise support ticket with evidence.

This workflow solves the majority of perceived **All Star Tower Defense X level rewards** problems without guesswork.
