## Understanding the All Star Tower Defense X Dark Wing Quest

The Dark Wing questline centers around the iconic character Ulquiorra (often referred to as Dark Wing in-game to avoid copyright issues). This NPC mission is divided into several stages, each increasing in difficulty and requiring more sophisticated unit compositions. The quest is primarily known for its "DPS checks," where the game evaluates if your total damage per second is high enough to clear waves before they reach the end of the track.

One of the most challenging aspects of the **All Star Tower Defense X dark wing quest** is Stage 2. This stage introduces specific elemental barriers that can render your most powerful units useless if you don't match them correctly. In ASTD X, elements play a much larger role than in previous iterations, and ignoring the color-coding of enemies is a surefire way to fail the mission.

## Stage 2 Strategy: The DPS Check

Stage 2 is where many players hit a wall. As noted by top-tier players, the enemies in this stage frequently appear with an **Orange** elemental affinity. To counter this effectively, you must bring units that utilize the **Green** element. Using the wrong element results in a massive damage reduction, making it nearly impossible to pass the DPS check.

The mission starts relatively slowly, but the density of units increases rapidly. Unlike standard story modes, the **All Star Tower Defense X dark wing quest** features a mechanic where enemies can spawn additional smaller units upon death or at specific intervals. This can quickly overwhelm your backline if you do not have sufficient Area of Effect (AoE) coverage.

## Step-by-Step Walkthrough

Follow these steps to ensure a smooth run through the **All Star Tower Defense X dark wing quest**:

### Phase 1: Early Economy
Focus entirely on placing your farm units. You may take some base damage early on, but in ASTD X, having a strong economy by wave 10 is more important than a perfect health bar. Place one cheap defender near the start to handle the initial weak mobs.

### Phase 2: Establishing the Kill Zone
Identify a "U-turn" or a long straightaway on the map. This is where you will concentrate your Green-element units. Ensure they are within the range of your buffer (Idol or Erwin). By wave 15, you should have at least two high-level attackers ready for the first DPS check.

### Phase 3: Handling the Spawns
As the video guide suggests, the Dark Wing quest often "just spawns more units" when you least expect it. To counter this, keep a reserve of cash to quickly place "Clean-up" units near the end of the track. These should be units with fast attack speeds and large AoE circles.

> **Warning:** Do not spend all your money on one single unit. It is better to have three moderately upgraded units covering different sections of the track than one maxed unit that can only hit one target at a time.

## Advanced Tactics for the Dark Wing Mission

For players struggling with the final waves of the **All Star Tower Defense X dark wing quest**, consider the "Double Buff" strategy. This involves using two different types of buffers whose effects stack. While this is expensive in terms of unit slots, it provides the firepower necessary to melt the high-HP bosses that appear at the end of Stage 2.

Another tactic is the "Elemental Pivot." If you find that the enemy elements change mid-mission (which happens in later stages of the questline), you must be prepared to sell your early-game units and replace them with the corresponding counter-element. This is why having a maxed-out economy is the foundation of any successful run.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the minimum level recommended for the All Star Tower Defense X dark wing quest?**

A: While there is no hard level cap, it is highly recommended that players be at least Level 50 and have at least one 6-star or 7-star Green-element unit to handle the Stage 2 DPS check.

**Q: Why am I failing the DPS check even with 7-star units?**

A: You are likely using the wrong element or failing to use buffers. In the **All Star Tower Defense X dark wing quest**, enemies have massive resistance to non-counter elements. Ensure your primary damage dealers are Green when facing Orange enemies.

**Q: Can I solo the Dark Wing quest, or do I need a team?**

A: Stage 2 can be soloed with a perfect loadout and high-level units, but it is much easier in a duo. Having one player focus on economy and buffs while the other focuses on elemental DPS is a winning strategy.

**Q: What are the rewards for completing the quest?**

A: Completing the various stages of the Dark Wing mission rewards players with evolution materials, gems, and eventually the shards needed to unlock or evolve the Ulquiorra (Dark Wing) unit into its final form.
