## Common Ascension Mistakes (and How to Avoid Them)

Even experienced tower defense players make these in ASTDX:

### 1) Farming without a target
You stockpile random mats but can’t complete a single ascension.

**Fix:** Lock one unit target first, then farm only what appears in that requirement list.

### 2) Ignoring harder challenge options
If your team can clear stronger variants consistently, lower-tier runs become inefficient.

**Fix:** Move up difficulty once your clear rate stays stable.

### 3) Upgrading too many units at once
Splitting resources delays every ascension.

**Fix:** Run a “one carry + one backup” investment pattern.

### 4) Confusing ascension and evolution
Some players gather evo materials for a unit they intended to ascend.

**Fix:** Confirm the exact upgrade path in the unit interface before grinding.

### 5) Not retesting team order after ascension
A newly ascended unit can change your ideal opener and placement timing.

**Fix:** Run 2–3 tests and adjust placement priority.
