<Section icon={Swords} title="Strategic Execution Steps">

Success in high-wave runs requires a disciplined approach to deployment and resource management. Follow these steps to optimize your Infinite Mode performance.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Loadout">
    Bring a balanced team consisting of an economy unit, a crowd-control unit, and at least two high-DPS main attackers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Early Wave Farming">
    Enable auto-skip to speed up early waves. Focus on maxing out your economy units to secure a stable income for the mid-game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mid-Game Transition">
    Disable auto-skip around wave 25-30. This allows you to manually manage unit placement and upgrades as the enemies become significantly faster.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Late-Game Survival">
    Use your crowd-control units to slow or stun enemies near the base. Prioritize upgrading your primary DPS units to handle the massive HP pools of later waves.
  </Step>
</Steps>
