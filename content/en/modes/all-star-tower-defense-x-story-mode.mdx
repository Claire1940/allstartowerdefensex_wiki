> **Warning:** Endless runs are inefficient if your upgrade timing is weak. Fix economy and wave control first, then farm long runs.

## Traits, Ascension, and Stat Potential: When to Invest

Traits and ascension are powerful, but timing matters. In **all star tower defense x story mode**, invest after your core team is functional, not before.

### Trait Investment Rule

- Keep high-value premium traits when rolled.
- Avoid dumping all rerolls into one unproven unit.
- Upgrade the units you actually deploy in story first.

| System | What It Does | Best Timing | Common Mistake |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Traits** | Boosts stats/effects | Mid progression | Overspending rerolls early |
| **Ascension** | Unlocks hidden upgrades + stat boost | After strong 5★ base | Ascending too many units at once |
| **Stat Potential** | Tree-based bonuses from dupes | Long-term optimization | Ignoring path synergy |

### Choosing a Stat Path

If your campaign issue is wave leaks, prioritize range/coverage nodes.  
If bosses stall your progress, prioritize damage/crit/penetration paths.  
If your unit has strong gimmicks (mark, ricochet, element advantage), build around that identity.

> **Tip:** One fully developed carry with thoughtful stat potential is usually more valuable than three half-built units.

For late-game **all star tower defense x story mode**, ascension is often the turning point that makes hard and endless farming more efficient.
