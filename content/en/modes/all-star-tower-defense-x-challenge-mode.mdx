<Section icon={Swords} title="Execution Steps for Success">

Executing a perfect run requires a disciplined approach to your economy and deployment. Follow these steps to ensure you maintain pressure on your enemies without running out of funds.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Farming">
    Deploy your money-farming units immediately. Do not place combat units until your income stream is established, unless you are in immediate danger of losing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Early Defense">
    Place a low-cost, high-efficiency unit to handle the first 10 waves. This allows you to continue funneling gold into your farming upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Support Deployment">
    Position your slow-effect units near the entrance of the enemy path. This maximizes the time enemies spend under fire from your DPS units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Scaling DPS">
    Once your economy is stable, begin upgrading your main DPS units. Focus on maxing out one or two strong units rather than having many weak ones.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Factor">
The key to deep wave progression is the "stacking" of effects. Ensure your slow-support units have overlapping ranges to keep enemies stuck in your highest-damage zones for as long as possible.
</Callout>
